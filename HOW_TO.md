# How to Use

## 1. Set the Data Directory

The app needs a directory containing `speaker-schedule.json` and `unit-schedule.json` to display schedules.

### Option A: From the banner

On first launch you will see a blue banner at the top of the screen. Click **Open Folder** to select the directory containing your JSON data files.

### Option B: From the menu bar

Click **Data** > **Open Data Folder...** in the application menu bar.

Once selected, the banner turns green and shows the directory path. The app will automatically load the data files and watch for changes (auto-reload is enabled by default).

## 2. Choose a Schedule

Use the **Schedule** menu in the application menu bar:

- **Speaker Schedule** - Shows stake council speaking assignments and ministering interviews, organized by speaker name with a table of contents.
- **Unit Schedule** - Shows ward/branch speaking assignments and ministering interviews, organized by unit ID.

## 3. Download the PDF

Both schedule pages render the schedule as a PDF using the built-in PDF viewer. The viewer toolbar appears at the top of the PDF area and includes:

- **Download button** - Click to save the PDF to your local filesystem.

The PDF includes a title page, table of contents, assignment pages, and instruction pages. You can click entries in the table of contents to jump to specific sections within the viewer.
